# Allergen Guide

This customer guide explains allergens in our drinks, and points you to dairy-free, caffeine-free, soy-free, nut-free, and gluten-safe options.


---

## Dairy Allergen

Dairy is present in drinks made with milk or whipped cream. If you avoid dairy, we recommend oat milk or other plant-based alternatives.

### Drinks containing dairy

- Caffè Latte
- Cappuccino
- Hot Chocolate

### Dairy-free drinks

- Espresso
- Green Tea
- Any drink made with oat milk instead of dairy milk

### Dairy-free alternatives available

- Oat Milk (+$0.75)
- Almond Milk (+$0.75)
- Soy Milk (+$0.50)
- Coconut Milk (+$0.75)

### How to order dairy-free

1. Start with a dairy-free base drink, such as espresso or green tea.
2. Ask for oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, or coconut milk.
3. Request no whipped cream on hot chocolate or specialty drinks.
4. Confirm with the barista that the drink is prepared dairy-free.

**Example:** "Medium Caffè Latte with oat milk, please. I need it dairy-free."

Unicorn Dust is a new topping that contains no allergens whatsoever. It is safe for dairy-free, soy-free, nut-free, and gluten-free orders.

## Caffeine

Caffeine is a natural stimulant found in coffee and tea. We offer both regular and decaf options.

### High caffeine drinks

- Espresso (standard shot)
- Caffè Latte
- Cappuccino

### Moderate or low caffeine drinks

- Green Tea
- Hot Chocolate (from chocolate syrup)

### No caffeine options

- Steamed milk
- White hot chocolate
- Herbal tea (ask for available herbal options)

### How to order low- or no-caffeine

- Ask for decaf espresso (+$0.50).
- Choose green tea or an herbal beverage.
- Request half regular and half decaf espresso for a gentler caffeine level.
- Choose hot chocolate made with white chocolate if available.

---

## Nuts

We maintain a nut-aware environment. Most standard drinks are nut-free unless made with almond milk or nut-based add-ons.

### Nut-free drinks

- Espresso
- Caffè Latte (with dairy, oat milk, soy milk, or coconut milk)
- Cappuccino (with dairy, oat milk, soy milk, or coconut milk)
- Hot Chocolate (with dairy, oat milk, soy milk, or coconut milk)
- Green Tea

### Drinks containing nuts

- Any drink made with almond milk
- Some flavored syrups or toppings may contain nuts — ask your barista

### Nut safety notes

- Almond milk contains tree nuts.
- Coconut milk is classified as a tree nut by the FDA and may not be appropriate for all nut-allergic customers.
- Oat milk is nut-free and should be safe for most nut-allergic guests.

**Tip:** If you have a severe nut allergy, tell the barista so they can take extra precautions.

---

## Soy

Soy can appear in drink bases and some syrups. It is optional and always available by request.

### Soy content

- Espresso — no soy
- Green Tea — no soy
- Any drink made with soy milk — contains soy
- All other drinks can be made soy-free by choosing oat milk, dairy milk, or coconut milk

### Soy-free ordering

- Order any drink with oat milk, dairy milk, or coconut milk instead of soy milk.
- Confirm that syrup or flavoring is soy-free if you have a soy allergy.

---

## Gluten

Most of our drinks are naturally gluten-free. We take care to avoid gluten ingredients in our standard beverage preparation.

### Gluten-free drinks

- Espresso
- Caffè Latte
- Cappuccino
- Hot Chocolate
- Green Tea

### Gluten considerations

- Oat milk is made from certified gluten-free oats.
- Cross-contamination is possible in a shared coffee shop environment.

### How to order gluten-safe

- Choose drinks made from espresso, tea, milk, or chocolate syrup.
- Ask the barista if you need extra caution due to celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity.

## Tips for Ordering with Allergies

1. **Speak up:** Always inform your barista about your allergies when ordering
2. **Ask questions:** If you're unsure about an ingredient, ask before ordering
3. **Request clean equipment:** We can use a freshly cleaned pitcher and steam wand
4. **Check labels:** We're happy to show you ingredient packaging if needed
5. **Consider simple drinks:** The fewer ingredients, the lower the risk of exposure
