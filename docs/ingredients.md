# Ingredients Guide

This guide covers the ingredients used in our coffee shop, including what each ingredient is, where it comes from, and what it adds to a drink.

---

## Espresso Shot

**Contains caffeine:** Yes  
**Contains dairy:** No

### What it is
A concentrated coffee extract produced by forcing hot water through finely ground beans at high pressure.

### Where it comes from
Our espresso beans are a blend of Arabica beans sourced from sustainable farms in Central and South America, roasted to bring out caramel and chocolate notes.

### What it adds to a drink
- Intense coffee flavor
- A rich crema layer
- Smooth body and texture
- A caffeine boost

---

## Milk

**Contains caffeine:** No  
**Contains dairy:** Yes

### What it is
Fresh dairy milk used for steaming and frothing in drinks like lattes and cappuccinos.

### Where it comes from
We use local dairy farms with humane practices and daily delivery to ensure freshness.

### What it adds to a drink
- Creaminess and body
- Natural sweetness
- Microfoam for latte art
- Mild texture that balances espresso

---

## Water

**Contains caffeine:** No  
**Contains dairy:** No

### What it is
Filtered water used for brewing espresso and tea.

### Where it comes from
Our water is municipal water treated with filtration and UV purification for consistent quality.

### What it adds to a drink
- Clean extraction of coffee and tea flavors
- Balanced strength and concentration
- Neutral taste that lets other ingredients stand out

---

## Chocolate Syrup

**Contains caffeine:** Yes  
**Contains dairy:** No

### What it is
A sweet syrup made from cocoa, sugar, and water, used to create hot chocolate and mocha drinks.

### Where it comes from
The cocoa is sourced from sustainably farmed cacao and processed into a rich syrup.

### What it adds to a drink
- Deep chocolate flavor
- Sweetness and richness
- Smooth mouthfeel
- A small caffeine lift from cocoa

---

## Green Tea Leaves

**Contains caffeine:** Yes  
**Contains dairy:** No

### What it is
Premium green tea leaves brewed for a light, refreshing drink.

### Where it comes from
Our green tea comes from high-altitude tea gardens in Asia, harvested for freshness and flavor.

### What it adds to a drink
- Light vegetal flavor
- Fresh aroma
- A gentle caffeine boost
- A clean, soothing finish

---

## Oat Milk

**Contains caffeine:** No  
**Contains dairy:** No

### What it is
A plant-based milk made from oats and water with a creamy texture.

### Where it comes from
Our oat milk is made from certified gluten-free oats sourced from sustainable farms.

### What it adds to a drink
- Creamy texture
- Naturally sweet flavor
- Excellent foam for espresso drinks
- A dairy-free alternative

---

## Ingredient Summary

| Ingredient | Dairy | Caffeine | What it adds |
|---|---|---|---|
| Espresso Shot | No | Yes | Bold flavor, crema, energy |
| Milk | Yes | No | Creaminess, sweetness, body |
| Water | No | No | Clean extraction, balance |
| Chocolate Syrup | No | Yes | Chocolate richness, sweetness |
| Green Tea Leaves | No | Yes | Light flavor, gentle caffeine |

*Ask your barista if you have questions about allergens or ingredient substitutions.*
