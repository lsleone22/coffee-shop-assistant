## Seasonal Availability Rules

- Seasonal drinks and flavors are offered for a limited time and may not be available year-round.
- Fresh ingredients such as certain tea blends or specialty syrups are subject to seasonal sourcing.
- The menu may change when inventory is low or during holidays.
- Customers should check with the barista for current seasonal offerings.

---

## How to Order Dairy-Free or Caffeine-Free

### Dairy-free orders
1. Choose a naturally dairy-free drink such as Espresso or Green Tea.
2. Request a milk substitute like oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, or coconut milk.
3. Ask for no whipped cream on Hot Chocolate or specialty drinks.
4. Tell the barista that the order must be dairy-free.

### Caffeine-free orders
1. Request decaf espresso for espresso-based drinks.
2. Consider a herbal tea or steamed milk for a fully caffeine-free choice.
3. Ask for half-caff if you want reduced caffeine but still want some coffee flavor.
4. Confirm the drink is made with decaf preparation.

---

## Substitutions

### Common substitutions
- Dairy milk → Oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, or coconut milk
- Regular espresso → Decaf espresso
- Espresso shot → Extra shot
- Flavor syrup → Alternative syrup or no syrup
- Whipped cream → No whipped cream

### Substitution rules
- Substitutions are welcome when ingredients are available.
- Some substitutions may alter preparation time.
- The barista may need to confirm availability of specialty ingredients.
- Exceptions may apply for seasonal drinks or limited inventory.

---

## Helpful Ordering Guidance

- If you have allergies, let the barista know before ordering.
- For dairy-free drinks, ask for oat milk as a first choice.
- For low caffeine, choose green tea or decaf espresso drinks.
- If you want a personalized drink, ask about customizations and substitutions.

*These rules help us keep orders consistent, safe, and easy to prepare.*
